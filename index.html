About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science, at National University of Singapore. I am fortunate to have Professor Prashant Nalini Vasudevan as my advisor. I am interested in topics related to foundations of cryptography, and my work mainly focuses on average case complexity and its applications in cryptography.

I also often play Capture the Flag (CTF) as a hobby, focusing on crypto challenges. I have another site for that.
